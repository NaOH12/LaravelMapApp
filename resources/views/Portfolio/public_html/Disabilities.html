Learning and Physical Disabilities

We provide:
  • Support for people living in their own homes or in shared housing
  • Live in services including 24 hours support
  • Day services and accompaniment for outdoor visits: shopping, etc.
  • Help with personal care, health and social needs
  • Domestic help including assistance with meal preparation, shopping and budgeting

In essence help with leading a full and active life – from 15 minutes to 24 hours per day, 7 days a week.
Our live-in services provide 24 hour support for the ultimate peace of mind. We provide 24 hour support with enablers on site responding to any need as required. This service provides exactly what its name suggests – a screened enabler will live in the home providing truly bespoke 24 hour support. Services can be almost anything from help around the home, to personal care or access to the local community.
A person centred approach
We recognise that everyone we support is different with their own experiences, interests and lifestyles. By getting to know each person we can develop a lifestyle plan which is uniquely tailored to your needs and helps to achieve your personal interpretation of what it means to live a fulfilling life. Companionship is hugely important so we always try to make sure that our clients can choose an enabler who matches their personality and interest. In this way we can help people live the life they choose.
You're in safe hands
Whether you're choosing support for yourself or someone you care about, you naturally want to be sure you are getting the best possible service. We ensure all our customers are safe, their needs are met and they feel protected.
Flexible services
Providing support in the home is all about delivering assistance when and where it is needed most. Our enablers can provide as much or as little support as required. We can support with anything from cooking, cleaning, changing bed linen and doing laundry, to daily personal care such as dressing, washing, personal grooming, administration of medication and access to the local community for shopping trips to restaurants, pubs, cinema, etc. While we understand the importance of these daily tasks, we also understand the importance of the companionship our enablers can bring. Not only do they provide a regular source of conversation and social interaction, but they can also assist our customers in taking up hobbies, pursuing personal interests and keeping in touch with friends and families. We strongly believe in providing support which greatly enhances – rather than interrupts – our customers' lives.
