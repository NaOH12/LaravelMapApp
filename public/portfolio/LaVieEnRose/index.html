About us

We are stationed in Tewkesbury, Gloucestershire – we are available however, all across the county.
The very best in quality care for the elderly, disabled and adults with learning difficulties in their own homes. We provide excellent support to help people maintain and retain their independence and dignity.

We are a small,
client focused
organisation.
Our idea for the company came when, after several years of experience in working in care in the UK we found that Nursing Homes are incompatible with our notion of care. Our tradition is to care at home; in our countries people close to us remain with us in our home.
Giving our elders care we pay off a debt of gratitude which has been done for us in the past. With us, no one is left alone and abandoned. Respect for you and excellence in our work is the foundation of our business.
